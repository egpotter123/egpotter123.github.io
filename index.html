Different Types of Dogs

There are many different species of dogs in the world. From huskys to shih tzus, all dogs range from different sizes and shapes. In this web page, some of the most common dogs will be discussed. Everything that is needed to know about them will be shown. 

-Emma Potter

Golden Retriever

-Goldens were first bred in Scotland
-As the dog ages, his fur will darken.
-Their sense of smells are among the best of all dog breeds.
-This breed has 2 layers of fur. The inner layer is to keep warm and the outer layer is waterproof.
-The golden retriever is a crossbreed. The breed originates from a Tweed Water Spaniel and a Yellow Flat Coated Retriever.

Golden Retriever dog laying down

German Shepherd

-German Shepherds are very smart
-They are super active and need a lot of running room
-German Shepherds are members of the Herding Group
-The average lifespan of a German shepherd is 9-13 years

German Shepherd dog

Shih Tzu

-The Breed Is More Than 1,000 Years Old.
-They Came From Tibet. 
-Their name means 'little lion' because their fur makes them look like lions.
-Shih Tzus are classified in the 'toy' dog group.

Shih Tzu dogs

Labradoodle

-Their lifespan is an average 12-16 years
-They are a crossbreed of a Labrador Retriever and a poodle. 
-They are used as guide, assistance, and therapy dogs.
-These dogs are hypoallergenic and do not shed a lot.
